Brand & Visual Guide

Bhavya Jain — Portfolio Design System

The single reference for keeping every future addition to the site — text, buttons, charts, 2D mockups, or the 3D globe — in the same visual language as index.html. If it's not on this page, match the nearest thing that is.

01

Color palette — 5 core colors

One neutral ink plus four vivid block colors, brightened and saturated — a nod to Marimekko's bold Finnish block-print style. Every section, chart, chip, and marker on the site draws from exactly this set.

Supporting neutrals (not counted in the 5): Background #ffffff, Muted #6b6b70, Line #ececeb, Panel #fafaf9 — used for structure only, never as a "brand" color.

How the color-blocking works Each major section is tagged with one color from the palette — Case Study & Journey stay purple (the primary/flagship color), Experience is green, Prototypes is yellow, and Strategy is pink (its whole section background is the pink tint block). The same rotation repeats inside components: the four Case Study quick-fact cards are each a solid tint block in this order, and the three Experience cards each get a matching top color-strip. Reuse this exact rotation for anything new — don't assign a 6th color to a new section.

07

3D globe spec

The globe is the one 3D object on the site. Keep its palette and behavior identical everywhere it appears.

Sphere Photographic texture (threejs.org earth_atmos_2048.jpg + specular map), with a procedural canvas fallback in matching ocean-blue/land-green tones if the texture fails to load. Two-layer additive atmosphere glow, faint blue, BackSide material. ~220-point starfield behind it.
City marker — #ec008cEvery visited city except home, in the palette's pink. Halo at 35% opacity, #ff8fd0.
Home marker — #1fa84aTampere only, in the palette's green. Slightly larger marker + halo, halo at 45% opacity, #7bdf9e.
Sizing
Footer showcase size: 240px canvas in a 260px column, presented as its own centered row below the contact links — not squeezed between them. Drag to rotate (pointer events), slow auto-rotate when idle, raycaster hover tooltip on markers.
08

Tone of voice

The site's credibility comes from being concrete and real. Every future line of copy should pass this filter.

Do

  • State real numbers and dates (35+ months, 12 countries, Feb–Aug 2022).
  • Say what's live vs. prototyped, explicitly.
  • Keep sentences short and declarative.
  • Credit real companies with real logos, or an honest placeholder if none exists.

Don't

  • Invent metrics, logos, or quotes that aren't real.
  • Use vague growth-hacker language ("revolutionary", "game-changing").
  • Stack more than one CTA color per section.
  • Add a 6th color instead of reusing purple, green, yellow, or pink.
