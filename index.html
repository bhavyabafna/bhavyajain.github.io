Flagship Case Study

Building YAC, alone

From a blank page to a live, revenue-generating business, solo — plus two AI features already designed for what's next.

YAC · Your Abroad Consultant yourabroadconsultant.com ↗

Study abroad is one of the biggest decisions a student makes, and almost nobody explains it in plain language

Admissions, visas, and financing, spread across dozens of institutions and government sites, in language built for bureaucrats, not 18-year-olds.

The problem

Study-abroad info is scattered and jargon-heavy. I built the plain-language place that explains what's next.

What's live today

A real consultancy at yourabroadconsultant.com: paid courses, real partnerships, real students who've won scholarships.

What's next

An auto-generated journey & study planner (home to new city — what to do, when, with reminders and progress tracking), plus an AI micro-loan eligibility checker approving up to €50 for day-one costs. Both designed and demoed in Figma; not live yet.

My role, solo

UX/UI, both AI prototypes, the website, marketing, and funnel optimization, as PM, designer, and tech lead at once.
